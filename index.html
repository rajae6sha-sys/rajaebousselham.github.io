Selected Research & Engineering Projects

Multi‑Layer Passive Adaptive Thermal Enclosures

Objective: Minimize year‑round energy consumption while reducing material usage across climates.

  • Integrated adaptive thermal conductivity, thermal diodes, smart heat storage systems, and adaptive radiative layers
  • Transient COMSOL multiphysics simulations with climate and application‑specific boundary conditions using different adaptability configuration levels
  • Multi‑objective optimization for energy demand and enclosure thickness
  • Experimental validation of optimized configurations

Impact: Achieved near‑zero annual energy consumption with overall thickness around 3.5 cm.

Skills: Heat transfer, multiphysics modeling, optimization, experimental validation

Bio‑Inspired SS‑PCM Radiative Coatings

Objective: Enhance passive thermal regulation using bio-inspired surface features.

  • Analyzed biological surface morphologies and their thermal–optical behavior
  • Built finite‑element COMSOL models with global optimization algorithms
  • Optimized surface features to minimize annual heat demand
  • Validated designs via nanoscale 3D printing, AFM, and FTIR measurements

Impact: Demonstrated tunable emissivity and improved passive heating/cooling performance tailored for three types of climates.

Skills: Thermo‑optical modeling, bio‑inspired design, photonic structures, light interactions, materials characterization

Machine Learning Surrogate Models for Passive Adaptive Thermal Design

Objective: Replace computationally expensive optimization workflows with fast predictors.

  • Developed neural networks and Random Forest models using ~1800 simulation points
  • Hybrid input features: material properties, geometry, and climate parameters
  • Regression‑based prediction of energy consumption and optimal thickness

Impact: Enabled rapid, personalized thermal enclosure design guidance.

Skills: Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, data analysis, ML regression

Industrial R&D – Saint‑Gobain

Objective: Improve optical performance and cost‑efficiency of polymer‑based products, and optimize pressure sensors for cost-performance efficiency.

  • Optimized filler type, size, and concentration for polymer films
  • Developed scalable formulations and experimental prototypes
  • Designed and validated high‑resolution pressure sensors under mechanical loading

Impact: Identified commercially viable filler–matrix combinations and improved cost efficiency while maintaining sensor resolution.

Skills: Polymer processing, optical characterization, sensor design, experimental testing
